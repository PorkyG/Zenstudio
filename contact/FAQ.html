Frequently Asked Questions
Find answers to commonly asked questions about ZenStudio's services, memberships, and policies.
What are your gym operating hours?
Our facility is open 24/7 for members. Staff are available from 6am to 10pm on weekdays, and 8am to 8pm on weekends.
How do I cancel or freeze my membership?
You can cancel or freeze your membership by visiting our front desk or through your online member portal. We require a 30-day notice for cancellations.
Do you offer personal training sessions?
Yes, we offer one-on-one personal training with certified trainers. You can book sessions through our website or at the front desk.
What classes are included in the membership?
All standard memberships include access to our group fitness classes including yoga, HIIT, spinning, and more. Premium classes may require additional fees.
Is there free parking available?
Yes, we provide free parking for all members in our dedicated parking facility during your workout sessions.
